About the Series

Solar is not just infrastructure. It is people.

Every Solar Story is a visit — a conversation with someone who decided to make the switch and lives with solar every day. They tell us why they did it, what changed, what surprised them, and what they would tell others.

We film in Sweden and Nigeria, because the contexts are different — and because that difference is exactly what makes the stories worth telling side by side.

SWE

Homes, farms, and businesses in Sweden sharing their solar journey.

NGA

Homes, hotels, SMEs, clinics, and communities in Nigeria powered by solar.
