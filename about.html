About Solardey

Built between two worlds.

A platform at the intersection of energy, storytelling, and opportunity — connecting Nordic innovation with Nigerian ambition.

The Founder

Prosper Emegoakor

Founder · Solardey

Based in Sweden with roots in Nigeria, Prosper brings hands-on experience from the Swedish solar industry and a strategic vision for connecting Nordic innovation with African energy markets.

He sees solar not merely as infrastructure, but as a catalyst for resilience, economic empowerment, and long-term progress. That conviction is what drives every conversation on the Solardey podcast, every Solar Story we film, and every connection we facilitate.

LinkedIn prosper@solardey.com
Our Mission

The energy transition is a collective endeavour.

It thrives when knowledge, questions, and experiences are shared openly — when discussions move from technical reports into homes, communities, and public spaces. Solardey exists to make that happen across the Nordic–Nigeria corridor.

We believe that solar is more than infrastructure. It is a tool for climate action, social development, and economic opportunity. Our approach bridges Nordic clean-tech thinking with Nigerian energy realities — practically, not theoretically.

What We Do

Three ways we add value.

Insight

We create content that informs, inspires, and opens meaningful conversations around solar energy and sustainable development across the Nordic–Nigeria corridor.

  • The Solardey Podcast — conversations with researchers, entrepreneurs, and policymakers
  • Solar Stories — YouTube series visiting real solar installations
  • Articles and thought leadership on market dynamics, policy, and practice
Explore the podcast

Connection

We connect the right people, ideas, and opportunities across borders. Through the Solardey Network, we facilitate introductions and relationships that wouldn't happen otherwise.

  • Partnership matchmaking between Nordic and Nigerian actors
  • Cross-border introductions based on alignment, not just availability
  • Ecosystem engagement through curated membership

Execution Support

For companies and institutions ready to act, we provide selected on-the-ground support. We help serious initiatives move with more clarity, confidence, and local context.

  • Market entry advisory for Nordic companies entering Nigeria
  • Partner and field verification — local validation of collaborators
  • Pilot project facilitation from idea to first execution
